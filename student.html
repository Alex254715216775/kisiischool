Our Student

The Kisii School is a full boarding school.
The Principal and most of the teachers are housed within
the compound.

The school presently accommodates the students
in eight dormitories (houses). Each of the existing
dormitories/houses is managed by
a housemaster.

Students are responsible for the daily cleanliness
of their dormitory. Once a week they are required
to undertake a thorough cleaning
exercise then inspection follows every
saturday morning.
